What can it do?

This tool can help you find your cursor when you feel like you lost it.

It helps teachers alot with online teaching.

Open the highlighter and a Yellow coloured circle will appear around it as soon as you do. You can change the color of the highlight and control how big it is.

Other capabilities

You can do these as well!

  • Display an image in place of the cursor.
  • Focus on the cursor by darkening the background.
  • Choose the color of the highlight when left and right mouse clicks are done.
