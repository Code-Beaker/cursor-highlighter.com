FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Cursor Highlighter 2.2

  1. What is the settings.ini file?

    A: It is a file that contains the settings of the highlighter script. You shouldn't modify it, unless you know something about it.

  2. Is it completely free?

    A: Yes. It is free for any use and forever. You can donate us to support the development of the tool.

  3. Can it display over start menu and taskbar?

    A: No, it cannot.

  4. Can it display two different colors for left and right clicks?

    A: Yes. You can enable it in the settings.
